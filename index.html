Frequently Asked Questions

Learn who we are, our passion for technology and how to contact many of the best developers for your site.

Who Are Handy Developers?

We are a group of specialized web developers with more than 10 years in web development, SEO optimization, Backend and Frontend development and more.

We have covered over the past decade multiple types of platforms, web development paradigms and patterns and even different ways of enhancing old and current sites for our clients in order to improve their services for their clients.

What services do we offer for your site?

Depending on your platform, we offer services that range from SEO Ranking Score (PageSpeed, YSlow, GTMetrix) to faster page loading, exporting member contacts, migrating member information, formatting data, to even extensive customization requests (Joomla Extensions, Wordpress Plugins, eDirectory Extensions and more).

We also offer many types of services based on the platform and ideas you have, to enhance your site and have better security, access and ease of use.

Most of the services are done in record time. Small jobs are done in less than an hour, while other jobs might take between a day to a month. From the minute the project starts we dedicate 100% of our time to your project (No shared time) and work to give you the most optimized, fast and working programming for your site.

Can I Ask for personal Website Customizations?

Yes. Most of our clients ask for customizations for their sites. All types of customization possibilities are opened and on most platforms, they can be a game changer and even enhance the experience your clients and developers have.

Do you offer dedicated software developers for hire?

Yes, we currently have several strong web developers for hire that can work for you and your sites. This is not the same as a Long Term Project where the Developer after finishing with all pending projects can work with other partners. For this case, the developer will exclusively work for you and your sites.

If you feel you would like to have a developer that takes care of your site, do backups, import and export members & data, do optimizations, create custom features and more, please let us know and we will put you in contact with one of your senior developers.

What Forms of Payment and Refunds are Available?

All of our services can be done through PayPal or through Payoneer. Our payments are flexible and depending on the project and timeframe for it, they can adapt to the client (The client can negotiate the terms).

For refunds, if you have a specific deadline with all the information regarding the project already submitted and the developers fail to meet the goal, we will do a full refund if the client wishes, although, typically developers are energetic, focused and fully supporting the project until finished, constantly notifying the partner about the work and any information needed (if any).

How can I Contact Handy Developers?

You can contact us by sending an email to Handy Developers.

We recommend when sending an email to also send us your name, customization request and what platform you are using in order for us to have a better idea on how to help.
