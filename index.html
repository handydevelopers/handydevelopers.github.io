Frequently Asked Questions

Learn who we are, our passion for technology and how to contact many of the best developers for your site.

Who Are Handy Developers?

We are a group specialized web developers with more than 10 years in web development, SEO optimization, Backend and Frontend development and more.

We have covered over the past decade multiple types of platforms, web development paradigms and different ways of enhancing old and current sites for our clients in order to improve their services for their clients.

What services do you offer for my site?

Depending on the platform, we offer services that range from SEO Ranking Score (PageSpeed, YSlow, GTMetrix) to faster page loading, to even extensive customization requests (Joomla Extensions, Wordpress Plugins, eDirectory Extensions and more).

We also offer many types of services based on the platform and ideas you have to enhance your site and have better security, access and ease of use.

Most of the services are done in record time. Small jobs are done in less than an hour, while other jobs might take between 5 days to a month. We dedicate 100% to your project and work to give you the most optimized, fast and working programmig for your site.

Can I Ask for personal Website Customizations?

Yes. Most of our clients ask for customizations for their sites. All types of customization possibilities are opened and on most platforms, they can be a game changer for your site and enhance the experience your clients and developers have.

Do you offer anonymous or private software development?

Yes, we offer anonymous/private software development for other developers, entrepreneurs, on-site and off-site developers and even site owners that would like to maintain privacy.

No credit is taken for a task and all documentation (if applicable) is delivered to the developer. All code will belong to the site owner or developer.

We also offer special services for site owners that use their time to service their site members, clients and more.

What Forms of Payment are Available?

All of our services can be done through our Invoice Services with PayPal or Payoneer (Recommended).
All services that use less than 2 development hours can be completed before any payment is received.

How can I Contact Handy Developers?
